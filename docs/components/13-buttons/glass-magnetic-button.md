### GlassMagneticButton

Button with magnetic hover effects and glass styling.

```tsx
<GlassMagneticButton
  onClick={handleClick}
  magneticStrength={0.3}
>
  Magnetic Button
</GlassMagneticButton>
```

**Props:**
- `magneticStrength?: number` - Strength of magnetic effect
- `onClick?: () => void` - Click handler
